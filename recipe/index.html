Chocoflan

This is the famous Chocoflan that has been passed down through history. My mom has made this recipe since she was 16 right before her mom passed away. It wasn't until I was 16 that I was introduced to this recipe.

Mixing the Cake

  1. Get half of the ingredients that are listed in the back of the cake mix
  2. Once you get half mix until no lumps are shown

Making the Flan

  1. Put five eggs inside a blender
  2. Put one can of evaporated milk and Condensed milk inside the same blender
  3. Add some Vanilla Extract

Putting it all together

  1. Start by putting your cake mix inside your mold
  2. Carefully put your flan mix inside your mold
  3. Once Everything is mixed, insert mold inside the oven for 450 degrees for 45 minutes
